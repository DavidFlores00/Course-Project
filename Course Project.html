The Resources of Southeast Community Academy

There are many challenges the people SCA seek to help have. Some of them consist of low income, lack of one or both parents, transportation issues, and low education attainment. Children with sufficient resources have always had access to excellent tutoring. However, pupils of color and those with low incomes have not always had access to the kind of focused tutoring that is effective. High-Impact Tutoring programs help raise low-income students' academic performance by providing tutoring to more children who need it and have less resources. Additionally, High-Impact Tutoring has a history of reducing the achievement inequalities among ethnic groups.

Solution

The high-impact tutoring system would address the life challenges mentioned above by students receiving specialized attention that is not available to them in a busy classroom. This benefits under-challenged kids as well as kids who have trouble keeping up. Additionally, it helps pupils stay on course when they have time off from school, like March Break or the summer.

About High Impact Tutoring

The high-impact tutoring system is an innovative approach to learning. High-impact tutoring refers to tutoring that has been shown through cutting-edge research studies to significantly increase student learning, or tutoring that possesses qualities that have been shown to speed up learning. High-Impact Tutoring adapts to each student's unique requirements and enhances the curriculum taught in the classroom.

Hight Impact Tutoring importance

High-impact tutoring has been shown to be 20 times more effective than standard tutoring models for math and 15 times more effective for reading. Research has shown that High-Impact Tutoring raises students' performance in the tutored subject and even in other classes by fostering a love of learning and enhancing their sense of growth mindset and self-efficacy.High-impact tutoring is important because individualized training and tailored social-emotional assistance can be provided to difficult students through tutoring. High-Impact tutoring raises students' growth attitude, sense of self-efficacy, and passion for learning, all of which have been shown to raise test scores in the subject being tutored as well as in other classes.
